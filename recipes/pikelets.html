Pikelets

Description

These are pancakes, right? I'm pretty sure they're pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 Tb of melted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients.
  2. Beat the egg and sugar.
  3. Add melted butter and milk.
  4. Stir in dry ingredients.
  5. Drop spoonful onto hot greased pan.
  6. Turn when brown.
  7. Wait until they are cold then apply butter or syrup or other topping.
