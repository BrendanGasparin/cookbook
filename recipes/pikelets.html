Pikelets

Description

Pikelets are like miniature pancakes, but are smaller and thicker. They taste great with butter, syrup, jam, whipped cream, and many other toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 Tb of melted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients.
  2. Beat the egg and sugar.
  3. Add melted butter and milk.
  4. Stir in dry ingredients.
  5. Drop spoonful onto hot greased pan.
  6. Turn when brown.
  7. Wait until they are cold then apply butter and/or other toppings.
